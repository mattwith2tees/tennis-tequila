FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to be good at tennis to join? +

Not at all! We welcome all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned players. Our events are designed to be inclusive and fun. We even have beginner clinics to help you get started.

How do I sign up for events? +

Once you've joined our community through the sign-up form, you'll get access to our Slack channel where we post all event details and RSVPs. You can also follow us on Instagram for announcements.

Do I need my own tennis equipment? +

It's helpful to have your own racquet, but we can sometimes accommodate equipment sharing for beginners. Just let us know in advance and we'll try to help out!

What's the age range of members? +

Our community is primarily young professionals in their 20s and 30s, but we welcome anyone who vibes with our culture and energy. It's more about mindset than age!

Is there a membership fee? +

Joining the community is free! Some events may have costs associated with court rentals or venues, which we split among attendees to keep things affordable and accessible.

Where are events held? +

We host events at various tennis courts and venues around the area. Locations are shared in our Slack channel for each event. We try to rotate locations to keep things fresh and accessible.
