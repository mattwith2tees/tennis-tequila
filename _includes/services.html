OUR VALUES

What we stand for and what you can expect from every T&T experience.

People First

Every decision starts with the member experience. Free water, spare racquets, gifted tickets for those who can't afford them. We take care of our people.

Accessible, Not Exclusive

Tennis has a reputation for being elitist. We're flipping that. No experience required. No membership fees. No attitude. Just show up.

Elevated Experience

We sweat the details. The DJ. The drinks. The venue. The vibes. Every event should feel like something you'd post about, because it actually was that good.

Community Over Competition

We're here to meet people, not keep score. Networking happens naturally when you're having fun together.

For the Culture

Atlanta energy. Young professional crowd. Good music. Good drinks. Good people. We're building something that feels like us.
