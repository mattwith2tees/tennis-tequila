The Tennis & Tequila Singles Championship at ITP

Event Proposal

July 25, 2026

Executive Summary

Tennis & Tequila is proposing a premier singles elimination tournament at ITP Training Academy on Saturday, July 25, 2026. This 32-player championship (16 men, 16 women) will feature a $10,000 prize purse and is projected to draw 500+ spectators for an unforgettable day of competitive tennis and community celebration.

We are inviting ITP Training Academy to be the Title Sponsor in exchange for waiving the $5,000 venue buyout fee. In return, ITP receives prominent naming placement, premium brand exposure to 500+ attendees, and positioning as the official home of Atlanta's premier amateur tennis championship.
