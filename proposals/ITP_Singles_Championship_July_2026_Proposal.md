# The Tennis & Tequila Singles Championship at ITP
## Event Proposal | July 25, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Tennis & Tequila is proposing a premier singles elimination tournament at ITP Training Academy on **Saturday, July 25, 2026**. This 32-player championship (16 men, 16 women) will feature a **$10,000 prize purse** and is projected to draw **500+ spectators** for an unforgettable day of competitive tennis and community celebration.

**We are inviting ITP Training Academy to be the Title Sponsor** in exchange for waiving the $5,000 venue buyout fee. In return, ITP receives prominent naming placement, premium brand exposure to 500+ attendees, and positioning as the home of Atlanta's premier amateur tennis championship.

#### VIP Tickets — $50
Premium experience with exclusive perks:

### GA Ticket Strategy: $25 with $15 F&B Voucher

Each GA ticket includes a **$15 food & beverage voucher** redeemable at ITP. This serves multiple purposes:

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Drives ITP minimum spend** | 350 GA × $15 = $5,250 guaranteed toward $7,500 requirement |
| **Enhances guest experience** | Every attendee gets to enjoy the bar/food |
| **Strengthens ITP partnership** | Guarantees F&B traffic ITP values |
| **Net ticket revenue** | $25 - $15 voucher = $10 net per GA ticket |

**Voucher Economics:**
- GA voucher liability: 350 × $15 = $5,250
- VIP/player organic spend estimate: $2,000+
- **Total toward minimum spend: $7,250+** (covers nearly all of $7,500 requirement)
