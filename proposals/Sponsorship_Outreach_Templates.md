## TIPS FOR OUTREACH

1. **Sound like a human** - Write like you're emailing a friend about something cool, not pitching a board room
2. **Lead with why you thought of them** - Makes it feel personal, not mass-blasted
3. **Give options, not ultimatums** - Let them choose what fits their budget
4. **Keep it short** - Busy people skim. Get to the point.
5. **Make the ask small** - "Open to a quick chat?" is easier than "Are you ready to commit?"
6. **Follow up** - Most deals happen after 2-3 touches
7. **Use the deck as backup** - Link it, don't paste everything in the email
8. **Track everything** - Know who you've contacted and when
