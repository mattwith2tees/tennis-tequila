### Registration Strategy
1. **Open registration in phases:**
   - Signature events: 2-4 weeks before
   - Monthly mixers: 2 weeks before
   - Clinics: Rolling registration (book month ahead)

2. **Create scarcity:**
   - "Only 10 VIP spots left"
   - "First 20 registrants get free merch"
   - "Early bird pricing ends Friday"

3. **Use waitlists:**
   - If event sells out, start waitlist
   - Auto-notify if spots open
   - Shows demand for future events

- **Monthly newsletter:** Full schedule + next month's events
