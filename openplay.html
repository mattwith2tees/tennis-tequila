How It Works

Beginners

Outdoor courts with a coach. Learn the basics, get tips, and play in a supportive environment. Perfect if you're new to tennis.

Int/Advanced

Indoor courts, open play doubles. Rotate in, play quick games, and meet new hitting partners. Competitive but friendly.

Rotation Format

Quick games with rotating partners. Everyone serves, everyone plays. No sitting on the sidelines for long.

Lounge Vibes

Not playing? Hang in the lounge, grab a drink, watch matches, and enjoy the DJ. Great for spectators and +1s.
