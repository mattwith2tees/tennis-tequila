How It Works

Beginners

2 outdoor courts with a coach. Learn the basics, get tips, and play in a supportive environment. Perfect if you're new to tennis.

Int/Advanced

4 courts (2 indoor + 2 outdoor). Open play doubles — rotate in, play quick games, and meet new hitting partners.

Rotation Format

Quick games with rotating partners. Everyone serves, everyone plays. No sitting on the sidelines for long.

Lounge Vibes

Not playing? Enjoy lawn games, trivia, karaoke, and the DJ. Grab a drink, watch matches, and mingle. Great for +1s.
